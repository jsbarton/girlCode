BOOLEAN OPERATORS


Congratulations on making it to this coding challenge! Boolean Operators sound complex, but its really just a fancy way to describe words like AND , OR, and NOT, that link two phrases together. They help us link two phrases together to specify a condition when one phrase isnt enough. This concept is best descibed with examples, so let's look at some below.

This flower is yellow.

This flower is yellow AND green.

This flower is NOT yellow.
