FOR-LOOPS


Congratulations on making it to this coding challenge! A for-loop is a way to "move" through a set of data. By data, we can mean any sort information a computer can use, for example, the squares you're moving through to complete your challenges! When completing a for loop, the computer processes the data for the amount of times you filled in the blank. For example, if I wanted to move 2 spaces to the right, I would have a for loop that reads: 

for  2  spaces:
  move right.
